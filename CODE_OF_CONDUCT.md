# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

- Harassment and disparaging comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Insulting or demeaning comments and personal attacks

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers via a new issue or direct message to the repository owners.
